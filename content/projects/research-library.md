## Overview

The Research Library is a self-hosted research dashboard built on top of OpenClaw, my 20-agent multi-agent orchestration system. It turns passive bookmarking into active intelligence gathering.

Drop in a link or a topic. OpenClaw's agents go to work — pulling relevant information, analyzing content, and delivering a clean, digestible summary back to the dashboard. No manual reading. No tab hoarding. Just structured knowledge, on demand.

![Research Library — Dashboard Detail](/research-library%20001.png)

## Why It Exists

In a field where the landscape shifts weekly, staying current isn't optional. The Research Library serves as a personal command center for tracking emerging AI trends, new tools, research papers, and industry developments in real time — all routed through infrastructure I built and maintain on my own hardware.

## What This Project Demonstrates

A responsive, mobile-first dashboard with a polished UI that looks and feels like a production-grade product, not a side project. It connects a visually intuitive frontend to real self-hosted backend infrastructure, including multi-agent orchestration, intelligent routing, and automated content processing. The result is a tool that isn't just technically impressive — but genuinely useful every single day.
